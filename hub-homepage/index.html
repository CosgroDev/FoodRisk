Why Food Risk

Built by someone who has lived the day-to-day reality

Food Risk is built by a solo developer with 10+ years working in food safety and quality departments, focused on process improvement, audit readiness, and practical compliance routines in real operations. The tools are shaped by direct experience of common pain points: fragmented systems, manual workarounds, and expensive software that forces teams into rigid workflows.

The goal is simple: provide modern, practical tools without locking businesses into mega-corporation cost profiles. Our modular model means separate subscriptions per product, so you can adopt only what you need, switch as your business evolves, and keep control of your stack.
